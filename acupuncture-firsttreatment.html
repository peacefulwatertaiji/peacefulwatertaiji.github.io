Preparing for your first treatment

It is important that you don't skip any meals before the scheduled treatment.

Please wear clean clothing with loose fitting tops and pants. You should be able to roll your sleeves or pant legs up comfortably without it pinching the arms or legs.

It is also important for the skin to be clean, or else the preparation of the needle site will not meet with Clean Needle Technique guidelines.
